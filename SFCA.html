About the project

This company has been in business since 1997 and is very well known in its local market. The website was built very early on, but was neglected for a long period of time. The old CMS system was completely out of date and many of the core functions ceased to function. I was charged with giving the website a more modern look and feel.

Challenge

Budget was a major concern as the website was relaunched during the slow period of the pandemic.

Solution

In order to modernize the look and feel while keeping the project under budget, we had to use a prebuilt theme. This allowed us to spend more time on editing old photos and bringin in new photos to help with the look of the website. New headshots were taken of the staff to showcase the Our team page and highlight the experience of the team.
